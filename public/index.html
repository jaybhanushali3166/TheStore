G.F. Ajgaonkar

गुंफ

Shri G. F. AJGAONKAR, popularly known as "GUMFA (गुंफ)", was renowned “Historian and Author”. He was a versatile man with numerous talents. He made outstanding contributions to the society in wide variety of domains. A Historian par excellence, Connoisseur of Literature, Visionary Writer, Emotional Poet, Fine Teacher, Fearless Journalist, Experimental Publisher, Smart Editor and Student of Law; “GUMFA (गुंफ)” left a legacy in each of the fields he touched. A humble man of many words, he earned respect and love of the society through his unbiased writing.As a writer or publisher, his was creator of Religious Literature on “Shree Dev Vatoba”, “Shree Dev Narayan”, “Shree Swami Samarth”, “Shree Raghvendra Swami”, “Dattagiri Gosavi”, “Chaitanya Swami”, “Shree Sadguru Sahajanand Saraswati”, “Hey Bhagwan”, “Soham Dhyan Tarang”, “Ovi Namamrut Saar”, “Shree Chaitanya Swami” etc.

गुंफ

His contribution helped many in their spiritual journeys. Through his literary work, he was able to highlight outstanding contributions of luminaries like “Swarabhushan Vasant Desai”, “Gunavant Dabholkar”, “Dr. Pandharinath Prabhu”, “V. R. Dhavle”, “G. L. Samant” (Tabla Maestro), “Natverya Madhavrao Walavalkar”, “Capt. M.K. Shinde alias Milind Madhav”, “INA Chief Senapati Bhosale” “Anant Shivaji”, “Guruvarya G.M. Divekar”, “Natvarya Parshuram Samant”, “Anand Yatri M.B. Mainkar”, “D.G.Tendulkar”, etc. to the society.His natural pursuit of historical research leads him to write books like, “Ajgaon Darshan” and “Walawal Darshan” which created a visual representation of these regions in his readers' mind. He was able to present the history of “Kudaldeshkar Gaud Brahmins” to the world.He talked about, wrote about and loved the enriching history till his last breath. Understanding his unfinished wish, his wife Mrs. Vinaya Ajgaonkar, a retired school principal, carried his research forward to author a famous novel "Kudaldeshkar Samagra Itihas".

गुंफ

This novel is used as reference material by many to study their enriching past. This book has solved many mysteries such as "Where do we originate", "Who are our ancestors" and most importantly "How knowing our past can help us build a better future together".Some of his outstanding creations of G.F. Ajgaonkar are: (1)“Shree Swami Samarth Stavan Gatha” consisting of 2288 Shlokas written by Swami Samarth Disciple Shree Anandnath Maharaj (2) “Prachin Marathi Sant Kavi - Part 2”, “Prachin Marathi Sant Kavi - Part 3” and also “Prasidha Purushanchya Aprasidha Gosti” written by Maharashtra Bhasha Bhushan Jagannath Raghunath Ajgaonkar (3) “Swanand Lahari” written by Shreemat Chidanand Swami (4) “Bharatatil Shastrancha Udgam ani Vikas, Manas Samajik Vichar ani Aaj Ghyavayache Dhade” and “Vedant, Dharma & Science” written by Dr. Pandharinath Hari Prabhu (5) “Shreemat Paramahanse Padmanabhatirth Swami Maharaj” a biography and religious writeup. (6) “A.B. Walavalkar” with Bhagwat Gita in Kudali Maharathi Dialect. (6) “Adnarthy Gita” (7) “Swanand Lahari” written by Shreemat Chidanand Swami, Baglanchi Rai
