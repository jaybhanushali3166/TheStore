About me

Mr. G. F. Ajgaonkar, popularly known as "GUMFA", was renowned historian and author. He was a versatile man with numerous talents. He made outstanding contributions to the society in wide variety of domains. A historian par excellence, connoisseur of literature, visionary writer, poet, fine teacher, fearless journalist, experimental publisher and a smart editor - GUMFA left a legacy in each of the fields he touched. A humble man of many words, he earned respect and love of the society through his unbiased writing. As a writer or publisher, his was creator of religious literature on Shreedev Vatoba, Shreedev Narayan, Shree Swami Samartha, Shree Raghvendraswami, Dattagiri Gosavi, Chaitanyaswami, Shree Sadguru Sahajanand Saraswati, Shree Vasudevanand Saraswati, etc. His contribution helped many in their spiritual journeys.

Author

About me 2

Through his literary work, he was able to highlight outstanding contributions of luminaries like Vasant Desai, Dr. Pandharinath Prabhu, Mr. V. R. Dhavle, G. L. Samant (Renowned Tabla Master), etc. to the society. His books like Jai Maharashtra, Ajgaon Darshan and Walawal Darshan created a visual respresentation of these regions in his readers' mind. Through his research, he was able to present the history of Kudaldeshkar Gaud Brahmins to the world. He talked about, wrote about and loved the enriching history till his last breath. Understanding his unfinished wish, his wife Mrs. Vinaya Ajgaonkar, a retired school principal, carried his research forward to author a famous novel "Kudaldeshkar Samagra Itihas". This novel is used as reference material by many to study their enriching past. This book has solved many mysteries such as "Where  do we originate", "Who are  our ancestors" and most importantly "How knowing our past can help us build a better future together".
